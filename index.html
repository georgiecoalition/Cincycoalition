About Us

The Cincinnati Coalition for Israel is a group of concerned volunteers whose goal is to bring together communities who support the modern state of Israel as the homeland of the Jewish people.

Why Are We Unique?

We strive to bring together persons of different faiths with a common love and admiration for the State of Israel.

Our Mission

We promote the well-being of the State of Israel as the independent Jewish homeland and oppose antisemitism and anti-Zionism in our community.

Core Values

  • We oppose violent expressions of antisemitism and anti-Zionism in our community.
  • We promote collaborations between different faiths to educate about Israel’s history and contributions.
  • We recognize Israel as the only true democracy in the Middle East, protecting human rights for all citizens.
